## Positioning

The honest hook is the **price + honesty contrast** with Sensor Tower / AppTweak /
AppKittie: self-hosted, free, MIT, and it *refuses* to fabricate the two figures
vendors model from private panels (dollar ad spend, panel-grade installs). The
federation angle (a shared HF anchor dataset that sharpens everyone's estimates) is a
strong "network effect for the little guy" story for Show HN.
